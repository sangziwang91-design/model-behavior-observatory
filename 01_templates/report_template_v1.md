# Report Template v1

## Page 1: One-line risk + Final decision

A concise statement of the primary observed risk and the final evaluation decision.

## Page 2: Scope

A short description of the tested system, task boundary, and observation scope.

## Page 3: Top 3 risks

A ranked summary of the three most important observable risks in the test round.

## Page 4: 3 failure cases

A compact presentation of the three most representative failure cases.

## Page 5: Repro

A reproducibility section that records the minimal steps required to re-run the observed behavior.

## Page 6: Recommendation / boundary

A final section summarizing recommended actions and explicit evaluation boundaries.
