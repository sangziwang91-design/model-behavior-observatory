# Public Surface Draft

This public surface is a compact window for **AI behavior evaluation**, **observability**, **red-teaming**, and **drift analysis**. It is designed to help readers understand what is being tested, how results may be structured, and where public-facing reporting begins, without exposing any private control core or internal operating logic.
