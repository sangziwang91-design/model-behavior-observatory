Reading rule

Do not ask only whether an answer sounds good.

Also ask: did the model clearly show where secure grounding ends?

That is the recurring question this public window is designed to keep visible.

Public stance

This site is meant to make a behavioral mismatch easier to inspect, not to claim a full remediation layer.

Readable, ordered, and bounded by release rules.
